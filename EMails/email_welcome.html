Game Points and Gold Dollars, what are they and how do I get them?

We'd like to give you an overview of the different ways to procure items in War Inc Battlezone. There are two different ways of getting cool stuff:

Gold Dollars - these are free
Gamepoints - the only thing you buy with real money

You use both of these currencies in a store to get new gear, armor, weapons, boosts and other cool stuff. While it's more convenient to use Gamepoints, you can still use Gold dollars instead for almost everything.

 

GD are earned simply by playing. (Stay until the end of a round to earn additional GD). Earn even MORE Gold dollars by completing Combat Missions or earning achievements and doing challenges. GD are free. Spend them all, you will get more, guaranteed. Gold dollars could be said to be there for those people who can't afford to spend a lot of real money, but have more time to play the game. GD CAN be used to buy yourself a real advantage in the game. I.e. you CAN buy bigger, better weapons with GD.

 

You can buy GP with actual money or you can receive them as bonus for leveling up, completing especially hard Combat Missions, receiving them as award for joining Clan. GP could be said to be there for those people who can't dedicate the majority of their time to playing the game. GP can be used to buy boosts, special items, Premium Account Status, gear and armor. Such items can offer an upgrade to your mercenary for performance and appearance.

 

Resupply and Black-Ops Crates

You might find yourself asking “What happens if I run out of ammo during a match?” well, no worries.  You have two options to satiate your needs.   The first option are Resupply Crates.   You will find these at any starting base.  Just approach one of these bad boys and press the “E” button and you will be resupplied with full ammo.

Your second option is the Black-Ops Crate.  The Black-Ops crates are conveniently scattered across battlefield in War Inc. Battlezone.  These crates are designed as fast and easy refills of ammo and can be accessed using a Black-Ops Key.  You can get the Black-Ops key by visiting the Store and purchasing it with GP or GD.

 

Advancing in the Game

War Inc. Battlezone has a ranking up system where your character will "level up" the more you play the game, you acquire Honor Points.  As you gain the needed amount of Honor Points to level up, you will reach a new ranking giving you a new Skill Point to then use in your chosen Skill tree.

Rankings.  Rankings are the games form of "leveling up" as you gain honor points your Honor or “experience” bar increases.  When you reach a full level of Honor, you are promoted to the next rank.

Honor Points (Experience Points). Honor Points are gained from killing enemies, capturing objectives and winning matches as well as by accomplishing other feats in a match. 

Skill Points. Are earned through ranking up.  These can be spent on skills to make your character  unique and powered up in matches. 

Abilities. Can be unlocked with Game Points through the abilities menu and will give you special abilities in game.  The abilities after being unlocked can be equipped via the Loadout menu.  You may only have 2 abilities equipped at one time

Skills. Once you reach a new rank, you will be allotted one skill point.  You can spend these skill points in the Skill menu to give your player unique skills, such as damage reduction, damage increases, explosion resistances, etc.

 

Call for Support

When you reach a certain level, you will unlock special item in a store that will allow you to  to use Calls for Support.  This feature allows you to call in Air Strikes, UAV’s, as well as other offensive and defensive abilities.

 

Jumping into a match

To enter a match head to the Play Game tab and click on Browse Games.  Here you will find matches you can join.  You will find there are things you can look for like ping, players in the map game mode, etc... in the menu body.

Conquest Mode The objectives of conquest mode are quite simple, help your team to capture nodes around the map.   The more nodes you have on the map, the faster the enemy teams resources spawn.  However, if the enemy has more nodes than you, you will be losing resources rapidly so it's important to work as a team to always be a step ahead of the enemy.

Deathmatch Mode Deathmatch is a simple team vs. team combat mode where each team is trying to get the most kills to reach the kill limit or score the most kills before the time runs out.

Customizing your character

You can customize your character by using your game points that you collect in game and/or purchase via the store.  These game points can be used to purchase things like new characters, gear and weapons as well as account upgrades.   Head to the store and check out some of the weapons,   items and purchase what anything you feel you would like.

After purchasing what you want head over to the armory and select a load-out you would like to equip your new items to.   After you have the load-out selected click on Modify.  To equip a weapon make sure you are in the equip tab and select on one of the weapon slots, like primary, secondary... etc.    To equip gear and custom models make sure you're in the character tab.  Make sure to save your load-out!

 

STORE

Select the STORE TAB. On the left menu there is the entire inventory of items for purchase such as ACCOUNT TYPE, GEAR, HANDGUNS, GRENADES, etc.. CLICK on the appropriate Category of the Item you wish to Purchase. The middle window will display all the available inventory, the right window provides specific detail and pricing of each item. Select GET ITEM in the lower right corner. You will then see a window detailing the pricing for the length of ownership - you may choose between these pricing options: 1Day, 7Days, 30Days or Permanent. Click on the window of the Price, and then select GET ITEM

CONGRATULATIONS - this Item is now in your inventory and can be equipped in the ARMORY

SPECIAL NOTE: Select the ITEMS category, you will see the BLACK OPS CRATE KEY. This key is very important, and once purchased, it does not need to be equipped, and will allow you to resupply from anywhere on the map that has a special Black Ops crate.

 

ARMORY

Select the ARMORY TAB, then CLICK on the LOAD-OUT you wish to MODIFY. CLICK MODIFY. Select EQUIPMENT, or CHARACTER on the left side of the page. On the EQUIPMENT there are the Weapon Types - these will swap between the Weapons you have Bought. The middle of the MODIFY screen will have a Green Arrow for the currently selected Weapon. The right side of the screen has PRIMARY, SECONDARY, HANDGUN, ITEMS, and ABILITIES. Select the Slot and then click on EQUIP. Select SAVE and then BACK to exit this menu. You can also press ESC to exit this menu.

 

SELECTING GAME

click on the PLAY GAME tab. if you have PREMIUM ACCOUNT you may CREATE a GAME. When you select CREATE GAME, you will be able to enter the Game Name, determine minimal rank, assign a password (if a private game), set # of players, # of rounds, Time Limit and Spawn Delay. Also when you CREATE GAME you will be able to select the GAME MODE and GAME MAP using the scrolling arrows to select. Once all the Game Parameters are set to your preferred option, click on START button in the lower right corner

BROWSE GAMES will allow you to view the available games. Filter your search options by selecting; Not Password, Not Full, Not Empty. REFRESH GAME LIST will update the list of available servers and games. Select the Game you want to join and Click START

QUICK MATCH. This will enable you to quickly join any random game based on the preferred modes and maps. Select the mode and map using the Arrow Scrolling. Click on START when you are ready to join

 

IN GAME (CROSSROADS MAP)

Crossroads is a Conquest Map that is won or lost based on how many Flags are Captured/Held and how many Enemies are killed

Each Team starts with 100 Tickets (points) - and as Flags are Captured, Tickets are Lost. The more Flags that are controlled, the faster the Tickets are Lost.

During the match you *may* run out of Ammo. To resupply you will have to run all the way back to your homebase. Or, if you have a BLACK OPS crate key, you can use this on the nearby crates.

To Capture a Flag - first find a flag and simply stand nearby. Watch out for enemies as they will be able to tell if a flag is being captured. The more teammates you have capturing a flag, the faster the capture process.

A match is concluded when the Time Limit is reached or one of the Team's Tickets reaches zero. Hopefully that it not your team! XP is earned for how many enemies you have killed, how many Flags you have captured and other great actions. Play well, and earn more XP!
